STAY OPTIONS

Growing old is a characteristic feature of all living creatures.

LONG TERM STAY

You have access to all amenities coupled with a serene ambience for an active lifestyle at THE REAL LIVING. Our housekeeping staff will take care of all your tedious chores. Your laundry will be happily taken care of when you don’t feel like using the Laundromat yourself. Indulge in leisure activities that uplift your mood at the indoor games area, the entertainment area, the wellness centre, the gym, the library or the business center. Relish in sumptuous meals prepared by our in-house chefs.

SHORT TERM STAY

. If you are looking for a old age housing community where you can live with peace and integrity, THE REAL LIVING is your best place to be at. TGE offers fully furnished living units with attached pantry and balcony, along with sumptuous buffet meals prepared by in-house chef and housekeeping staff to take care of all daily chores. You are also open to a highly advanced security system throughout the facility and round the clock medical assistance.

POST OPERATIVE CARE

Although these are the golden years for people, there might come times when things can get a bit dull to say the least due to reasons such as illness, physical injury or mental stress. Which is why here at THE REAL LIVING, we offer the best Post-Operative Care to help you cope up with the after-effects of a physical injury or illness that impacts your ability to carry out your daily activities. We offer such services in case of Cardiovascular and Thoracic Surgery, Orthopedics, Neurology, Oncology, and Ophthalmology and ensure the proper recovery and treatment of the aged.

ASSISTED CARE

Old-age is a time when a person is supposed to enjoy life since all the responsibilities have been completed but there are times when ill-health and misfortune can wage a war against the body and mind. But at THE REAL LIVING, we have Special care program for physically injured or ill patients. Our 24 x 7 In-house Medical & Physiotherapy team takes care of all daily needs. So live your life here while we take care of the rest.


Make the most of your old age at
THE REAL LIVING.
