FREEDOM TO PERSONALISE

While we provide soft furnishings, residents are free to bring their own and customise their unit to their liking and preference within expectable limits.
The balcony is furnished with relaxing outdoor furniture and the washroom has an anti-skid flooring, grab bars and emergency alarms to ensure our residents’ safety.

YOUR PERSONAL SPACE

At THE REAL LIVING, you can choose a living unit based on your preference from the following two formats:
• Suite
• Studio

A suite has a separate living room and bedroom, offering you privacy and more personal space, while a studio is more compact with a combined sitting and sleeping area.

PLUSH LIVING

Living units have been conceptualised to offer maximum comfort and functionality – fulfilling all your requirements – but not too large in scale, so that you lead a luxurious lifestyle at a minimum cost, within the urban centres.

Each living unit has an attached pantry, balcony and toilet, and is furnished with:

    *Double bed                                 *Sitting couch                         *Telephone line and Internet
    *Flat-screen TV                           *Geyser                                    *Writing table
    *Built-in cupboard                       *Emergency ‘Call-Point’
